  • What Is Racism?

    Racism is defined by the Merriam-Webster Dictionary as a belief that race is a fundamental determinant of human traits and capacities and that racial differences produce an inherent superiority of a particular race. Photo by Jeff Siepman on Unsplash

  • Microaggressions

    Microaggressions are subtle, sometimes unintentional comments or actions that have a negative connotation to marginalized groups. Microaggressions can be heard in every day conversations and examples include things like assuming someone is not from America, saying you don't see color, or saying things like "You're pretty....for a brown girl". More information can be found here.

  • Racism is very common

    Racism occurs everywhere, from schools to workplaces to social media, most poc experience some kind of racism throughout their life. Places like social media are one of the most common places it occurs since people can use fake accounts and say whatever they'd like while hiding behind a screen.
